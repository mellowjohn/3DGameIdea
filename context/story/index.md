## Continuity Status

The documents above capture developing narrative material. Statements labeled **established** align with the current story context. Statements labeled **draft** or **proposal** preserve ideas that require review before becoming canon.

Important unresolved continuity questions are collected in each document rather than silently resolved during import. Authors and agents: apply the [Continuity Checklist](continuity-checklist.md) on every story edit.
