# Companions

- Status: developing story and gameplay context

## Companion Structure

The initial companions are introduced during the tutorial and remain important to both the main story and player choice. Additional companions unlock through decisions made in the world.

The party proposal includes:

- Up to three active companions.
- Personal loyalty and morality reactions.
- Companion departure or recruitment based on player behavior.
- Permanent companion death in hardcore mode.
- A campsite party view showing companion slots, bonuses, morality synergies, and a shared stash separate from personal inventory.

## Arkand

Arkand is a knight from the Kingdom of Tessera. He acts as a guide and source of emotional support for the protagonist. Goofy, charismatic, and persistently bright in a dark world, his humor contrasts sharply with his imposing full armor and battlefield competence.

- Narrative role: guide and emotional support
- Personality: charismatic, optimistic, goofy
- Mechanical roles: tank and damage support
- Creative references: Puck from *Berserk* and Patrick Star-style humor; references are directional, not setting canon

Vanessa is a mage educated at the enlightened arcane academies in central Tessera. She views the world literally, takes most matters seriously, and prioritizes action over allowing the protagonist to remain stuck in self-pity.

Player choices involving the Cristallo and tribal factions shape her development. She may become an increasingly power-driven spellcaster or learn to open herself emotionally to others.

- Narrative role: pragmatic counterweight to Arkand
- Personality: literal, serious, forceful, action-oriented
- Mechanical roles: damage or healing support
