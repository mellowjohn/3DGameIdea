This site is the archive. LinkedIn is for reach.

**What you'll see here**

- Short build notes tied to real milestones (streaming, collision, World Forge, tooling).
- Occasional deeper write-ups on architecture or working with AI collaborators.
- Honest status. When something is still planned, it stays marked planned.
- Enough explanation that a sharp non-engine reader can follow along.

**How to follow**

- Share individual articles when a piece is worth a wider conversation.

I will stay open about how I use AI on this project. If a post gets deep into engine guts, I'll teach the vocabulary instead of assuming you live in C++ all day.
